BAS STUDIO — A STUDY IN MOTION

A STUDY IN MOTION is a chronophotography relief installation by BAS STUDIO: a single runner photographed in profile and broken across depth, milled into a stone-like panel and read by raking light, with a depth-mapped figure projected onto the relief in real time.

BAS STUDIO designs, builds, and deploys physical work — CNC sculpture, CAM fabrication, projection mapping, and realtime TouchDesigner media. A studio by Conner Ward and Nick Eschen. Installed at Gray Area Grand Theater, San Francisco — April 7, 2026.
